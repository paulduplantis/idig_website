üîç Featured: Building with User Agency

One of the core principles behind iDIG is designing tools that respect and enhance user agency. But what does this really mean in practice?

User agency means: Users maintain control over their data, attention, and digital experiences. They choose how to engage, what to save, and how to organize their digital knowledge.

This month, we're exploring how small design decisions can have profound impacts on user autonomy. From data ownership to attention design, every choice we make as builders either empowers or undermines the people using our tools.
