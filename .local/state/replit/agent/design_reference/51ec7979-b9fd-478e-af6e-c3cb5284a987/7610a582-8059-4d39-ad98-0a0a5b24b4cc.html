About iDIG

Empowering deeper engagement with digital content

Our Mission

At iDIG, we believe that the web should be a place of deep learning and meaningful discovery. Our browser extension transforms passive browsing into active engagement, helping curious minds extract more value from the content they encounter online.

What We Do

We create resonant tools that amplify your natural curiosity. Whether you're researching, learning, or simply exploring, iDIG provides the framework to dig deeper, make connections, and build lasting understanding from your digital experiences.

The Vision

We envision a future where every web interaction becomes an opportunity for growth and discovery. By providing thoughtful tools for content engagement, we're building a community of resonant builders who actively shape their learning journey.
