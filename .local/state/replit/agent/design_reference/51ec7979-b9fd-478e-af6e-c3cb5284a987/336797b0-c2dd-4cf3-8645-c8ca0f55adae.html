About iDIG

iDIG was born from a simple belief: the web should be a place for deep engagement, not just passive consumption.

In an age of information overload, we've created tools that help curious minds dig deeper into the content they encounter online. Our browser extension transforms any webpage into an interactive canvas where you can annotate, highlight, and connect ideas across the vast landscape of the internet.

Whether you're a researcher building knowledge networks, a student making connections between concepts, or simply someone who wants to engage more meaningfully with what you read online, iDIG provides the resonant tools you need.

Join us in building a more thoughtful web, where every piece of content becomes part of your personal knowledge ecosystem.
