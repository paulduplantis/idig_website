About iDIG

Empowering deeper engagement with digital content through thoughtfully designed tools

Our Mission

In an age of information overload, iDIG.io provides the tools to transform passive consumption into active engagement. We believe that deeper interaction with content leads to better understanding, retention, and insight.

Our browser extension and web platform enable users to annotate, connect, and build upon the content they encounter, creating a more meaningful relationship with information.
